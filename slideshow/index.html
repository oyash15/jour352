Olivia's Favorite Animated Movies

These are some of my all-time favorite animated movies, ones that often do not get as much credit as they perhaps should. Each of these films have shaped my humor and creativity one way or another, and I find myself quoting them or listening to their scores often. I resent the idea that movies made more children shouldn't be respected or held to the same regard as others. While all these films listed appear quite silly, they all have important messages and touch on themes of finding and accepting your purpose and doing what is right.
