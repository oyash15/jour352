Taylor Swift GRAMMY History

52 nominations. 14 wins. 4 Album of the Year awards. Taylor Swift is an artist that needs no introduction. From skipping her senior Prom to attend her first GRAMMYS in 2008, to annoucing her 11th studio album during her fourth Album of the Year win in 2024, Swift has had a plethora of iconic moments at the annual music awards show. Dynamic performances, stunning fashion choices and shocking album announcements have been a part of her GRAMMY reputation for many years.
